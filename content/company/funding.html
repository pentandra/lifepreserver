Funding

We could not have made it this far without the generous contributions and support of many. From family and friends to complete strangers to anonymous donors, we have been blessed by amazing people that believe in what we're doing.

We are so grateful.

Our Funders

Utah Individual Development Account Network

The first bit of seed money from the Utah Individual Development Account Network (UIDAN) started us off. We had the idea of developing a cool tool for genealogical research, and our initial business plan was based solely around this idea. UIDAN worked with us to perfect this business plan, and we were able to raise enough money to get organized and start working.

Anonymous Donor #1

Shortly after the Grow America Competition in 2012, we received an anonymous donation. We blog about it here. Among other things, we were able to invite more beta testers for Geungle and introduce Open Booth Chats at an NGS conference in Cincinnati, Ohio.
