Geungle

Geungle, pronounced like "jungle," is a platform for scholarly genealogical research, sometimes referred to as generational history. Designed to work the way professional genealogists work, Geungle allows collaboration in a way that has never before been possible.

Built upon standards from the digital archive, library, knowledge management, semantic web, and genealogical communities, Geungle is designed to interoperate at many levels to make genealogical research discoverable and research collaboration a modern reality.

Our Weltanschauung is Wide Open

Weltanschauung, or world view, has special meaning in genealogical research.

[The closed] world assumption implies that everything we don’t know is false, while the open world assumption states that everything we don’t know is undefined.
