Our Values

Passionate

Some people call it OCD; others call it passion. We love good research. We want to make it easier to do good research. We believe that software should work the way researchers work, not the other way around. That is what drives us---crazy.

Quality Over Quantity

We figure that if you're going to spend the time to do research, you might as well do it right. We want to make it easier for you to do <%= link_to('good research', '/research/') %>. And after you've done a great job, we want to help you show it off. (You know you want to.)

Small And Focused

We're small...and we like it that way. Being small helps us to move faster and be more responsive to the needs of our customers. It also helps us keep our expenses down. Even when we cross the divide from a struggling startup to a profitable company (hopefully sooner than later) we want to stay small and connected.

We Eat Our Own Dogfood

Necessity is the mother of invention. At least it was for us. We're genealogy junkies and needed something that would help us keep track of our family history research. In our hunt, what we found was a surprising lack of software that really understood the research process (in genealogy or any other research field). So we decided to make it ourselves. We thought it would be selfish not to share it, so here we are!

Committed

We are committed to making research easier. This is our primary objective. We also want to keep data open. To do this we are building on open standards. We are trying to accomplish something great. We are determined to see it through---for the long haul.

Open

Openness is all about collaboration. For us, this means open research, open source, and an open business model. We wouldn't be here without the open contributions from many in the technological and genealogical communities. We are happy to have a chance to <%= link_to "contribute back", "/open/" %>. We invite you to join with us to build something amazing together.

Our Mission

We are a <%= link_to "social entrepreneurship", "/blog/a-social-business/" %> company with a mission to preserve and accelerate the process of research. This is a researcher-driven effort to shift the paradigm of research, remove barriers to knowledge dissemination, and make research more accessible to the world.

True open access to research is currently held hostage by the limitations of the research paper publication paradigm. We want to empower researchers to preserve their entire research experience with clarity. Doing so enables researchers to collaborate and publish their research in context throughout the research process.

We have set out to achieve this mission through the following means:

  • Developing open-source tools to make open, transparent, real-time, collaborative, cross-domain research possible.
  • Promoting, creating, implementing open standards that further open research.
  • Helping individuals and organizations transition to policies and incentives that reward open research, providing consulting services and technical support, when needed, similar to what B Lab has done for corporations.
  • Promoting open research in general, it has benefits over enterprise, silo approaches.
  • Lowering the barrier of entry for the common man and practitioners to participate in high-quality research projects, throughout the research process.

We are a benefit corporation, which basically means that we're legally required to create a material positive impact on society and the environment. We go beyond that minimum legal requirement by establishing a non-dividend stock policy and reinvesting 100% of our profits in ways that will promote open research, build up the community, improve our products and services, or in some other way entirely---whatever will further our mission the most and maximize our social impact in the world.
