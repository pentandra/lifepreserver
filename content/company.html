Our Values

Passionate

Quality Over Quantity

We love good research. We figure that if you're going to spend the time and money to do research, you might as well do it right. We've seen (and produced) enough bad research that we know it is worth doing things right, even if it takes longer. We want to make it easier for you to do good research. And after you've done a great job, we want to help you share it and show it off. (You want to show it off after you've put all that work into it, don't you?)

We're Small

And we like it that way. Being small helps us to move faster and be more responsive to the needs of our customers.

The Mother Of Invention

Our company came about because we wanted to keep track of our own family history research. We hunted for software that would help us with it. We couldn't find any. So we decided to do it ourselves, and here we are!

We Are Here For You

We believe that software should work the way researchers work, not the other way around. We are here to simplify your research experience. Please let us know how we can serve you better! Really. We want to know.

We Love Community

Our company would not be here without the contributions from many in the technological and genealogical communities. We are happy to have a chance to contribute back.
