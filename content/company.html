Our Values

Passionate

Some people call it OCD; others call it passion. We love good research. We want to make it easier for you to do good research. We believe that software should work the way researchers work, not the other way around. That is what drives us. Crazy.

Quality Over Quantity

We figure that if you're going to spend the time to do research, you might as well do it right. We want to make it easier for you to do good research. And after you've done a great job, we want to help you show it off. (You want to show it off after you've put all that work into it, don't you?)

We're Small

And we like it that way. Being small helps us to move faster and be more responsive to the needs of our customers. Please let us know how we can serve you better! Really. We want to know.

We Eat Our Own Dogfood

Our company came about because we wanted to keep track of our own family history research. We hunted for software that would help us with it. We couldn't find any that did what we needed. So we decided to do it ourselves, and here we are! We thought it would be selfish to not share it.

We Are Here For You

We are committed to sticking around.
